PROCESS +
GUIDELINES

ACCREDITATION STEPS

  • Submit an Application of Intent.
  • Upon approval of your application of intent, the applicant completes and submits an Accreditation Application wtich is essentially a self-study Instrument.
  • The ACCI Review Team (trained practitioners) visit the campus of the applicant.
  • The Accredrtation Council considers the ACCI Review Team's recommendation and votes upon granting accreditation.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

  • The program must have been in continoous operation long enough to have graduated one class of students in the program.
  • The program must be used at a regionally accredited post-secondary institution.
  • The application fee (non-refundable) must be submitted through PayPal.
  • Program Self-Assessment

The completed application is sent to the Executive Director of the Accreditation Council. The application, including the application form, the profile data sheet, the Affirmation of Facts form and support documentation in appendices should be emailed and any required payment should be submitted through Paypal. Contact the Execuative Director for Paypal information.

Application will be reviewed by the Executive Director to ensure that the application is technically caomplete. If not complete, the applicant will be notified by email that there is a specific omnission(s). An application for accreditation will not proceed until the applicant has submitted the required materials: Affirmation of Facts form and application fee. Once all the documentation and fees have been recieved, the application is sent to the review team by the Executive Director of the Accreditation Council.
